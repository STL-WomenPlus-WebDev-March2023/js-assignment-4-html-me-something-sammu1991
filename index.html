My First Tasty Mushroom Curry

Mushrooms are not common in our house kitchen but they are widely used to make many dishes like Biryani, curries etc. If anyone asked me how good I am at cooking I would say “I can manage”. But yesterday night I was so proud of myself for making a yummy mushroom curry. I was so happy and satisfied that my family ate more than what they usually do. They enjoyed it a lot. It felt exactly like when I accomplish my graded assignments on my own. It gave me a lot of motivation to try new dishes in future. I am glad I am able to share these thoughts in this assignment :).

I have tried to remember the steps and write it as a recipe. Hope you enjoy.

What to serve with Mushroom Curry?

Serve Mushroom Matar masala with:

  • Indian bread like rotis, naan, parathas, or poori.
  • Basmati rice, jeera rice, quinoa, coconut rice, or cauliflower rice with Indian cucumber salad or pickle on the side.
