  16. Salt
  17. Dried fenugreek leaves
  18. Water

Preparation Step by step

  1. Rinse and cut mushrooms in thin slices.
  2. Grind onions, ginger, garlic, cashews, yogurt.
  3. Take a wok and fry whole spices like cardmom, cloves etc.
  4. Next, fry the ground paste until oil separates.
  5. Add the mushrooms and mix it gently.
  6. Pour some water and cover the wok and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Put some cilantro and the dish is ready.
